What changed

New Nationality Law — April 2026

On 1 April 2026, the Portuguese Parliament approved a major revision of the Nationality Law, introducing for the first time a mandatory civic knowledge test for naturalisation.

  • Legal residence of 7 years (CPLP/EU) or 10 years (others)
  • Proof of knowledge of culture, history and national symbols
  • Proof of knowledge of rights, duties and political organisation
  • Solemn declaration of adherence to democratic principles
  • Portuguese-speaking citizens: exempt from language test, but not from civic test

The ministerial order defining the test format will be published after promulgation — but the topics are already known and defined in the law.
